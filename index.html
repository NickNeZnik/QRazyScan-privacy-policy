Privacy Policy for QRazy Scan

Privacy Policy This privacy policy applies to the QRazy Scan app (herein referred to as "Application") for mobile devices that was created by Mikalai Skrobat (herein referred to as "Service Provider") as a Free service. Information Collection and Use The Application uses the camera device to scan barcodes. The Application does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data, images from the camera, or scanned barcode information to any external servers. All processing is performed locally on the user's device. Third Party Access The Application uses Google Play Services (ML Kit) for barcode recognition. You can find their privacy policy here: Google Play Services Privacy Policy. Data Retention Policy Since the Application does not collect any data, there is no data retention or deletion policy required. Contact Us If you have any questions regarding privacy while using the Application, please contact us via email at qrazyscan@proton.me.
